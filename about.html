About Us

Built out of frustration with broken tables

I was spending 20 minutes every day reformatting AI-generated tables. They looked perfect in ChatGPT and Claude — but the moment I pasted them into Word, the formatting was completely destroyed.

The reason? Copy-paste from a browser only transfers plain text. Word doesn't know it was a table. So I built Tabifi — a focused tool that reads the screenshot with GPT-4o Vision and copies it using the text/html clipboard format, which Word and Google Docs understand perfectly.

It's a small tool, but it solves a real problem for thousands of people every week.
